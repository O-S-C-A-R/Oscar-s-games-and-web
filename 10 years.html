10 Years Later

Honestly, I have no idea where i'd be in 10 years. I may be a homeless guy, I may be a coder, I may be a doctor, I may be a street musician, but who knows. The fact that I don't know what I'll become excites me. I don't know where life will take me in the future, and I'm going to leave it as it is. Where ever I am in 10 years doesn't really matter to me, what matters to me now is what happens now.
